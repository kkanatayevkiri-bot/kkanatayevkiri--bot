About Pokémon types

All Pokémon creatures and their moves are assigned certain types.
Each type has several strengths and weaknesses in both attack and defense.
In battle, you should use Pokémon and moves that have a type advantage over your opponent; doing so will cause much more damage than normal.


A single-type advantage (for instance a Water attack against a Ground-type Pokémon) will net you double normal damage.
The advantages also stack up, so a double-type advantage (for instance a Water attack against a Ground/Rock-type Pokémon) will net you quadruple damage.
In both these cases you will see the message It's super effective! in-game after the attack.
